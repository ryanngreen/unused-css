Admissions

Student Centered
At its heart, Southeastern is all about you, our student. Since its earliest days it has been a university focused on you and your future.
Strong Academics
Because we are built around our students, our academic programs never settle – we strive for excellence each day. Excellence that will propel your future.

With 14,500 students, Southeastern is large enough to offer a full engaging college experience and an expansive range of academic programs of study – with a personal touch. We offer a welcoming, caring environment with second to none academic programs that is built around serving our students. We are here for you, because you matter here.
